Are you ready for one of the most exciting stages of the Squid Game ? Try your best to win $38M and enjoy the rest of your life not worrying about a thing!

Welcome to the Squid Game Glass Bridge Stage. In this game, you will have to get pass a glass Bridge that combines of either a regular glass or a tempered glass in the same row. You will fall and lose one of your characters if you step on the regular glass. Your goal is to find the positions of all the tempered glass and reach the finishline. You will have 3 seconds to look at all the positions of the regular glass. Be sure to reach the finishline without losing all your three characters in order to win the game!
